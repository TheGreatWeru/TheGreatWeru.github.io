Earning Through Referrals

Active members can earn on MoneyFarm through referring friends using their own unqiue codes.
There is no limit to how much you can earn using MoneyFarm. There are various ways that you can earn using MoneyFarm. For information about ways of earning using MoneyFarm click here. In this section, we are going to focus with earning through referrals. You need just three referrals to gain back the money you used for activation, isn't that easy? If you cannot get as much as three people, don't worry. There are other ways of earning.
The following is referrals earning structure used by MoneyFarm to determine how much you have earned.

  1. Level 1
    • Invitation limit: 100 People
    • Amount per referral: Ksh. 200
  2. Level 2
    • Invitation limit: 200 People
    • Amount per referral: Ksh. 100
  3. Level 3
    • Invitation limit: 500 People
    • Amount per referral: Ksh. 50
  4. Level 4
    • Invitation limit: 100,000 People
    • Amount per referral: Ksh. 10
